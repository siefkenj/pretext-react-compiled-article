Remember that <sidebyside> has attributes that strongly influence layout. That is intentional. But to support a variety of output formats, it does not allow overly-precise control, and they be viewed as providing hints to an implementer of a conversion. So for example, do not expect <sidebyside> to function like a LaTeX tabular or an HTML table.
In particular, elements of two consecutive <stack> will not line up, unless perhaps you construct them identically. Consider a <sbsgroup> for something closer to putting items into rows.
