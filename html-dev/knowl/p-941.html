We support videos from Vimeo 3  in much the same way as YouTube videos. Now the identifier is a long integer. For example, the video up next would normally be found at https://vimeo.com/27246366. But instead, you can embed the video with as little as <video vimeo="27246366"/>. As of 2019-05-18, we intend to support as many of the options described above as possible. Widths and heights (via the aspect ratio) will perform as expected. We have not investigated start and end times.
